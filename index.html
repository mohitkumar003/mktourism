About Us




Thanks to its incomparable cultural and historical heritage, and landscapes as varied as they are splendid, India is a destination that never disappoints! And Hyderabad (HYD) is no exception! If you want a relaxing holiday without setting aside your favourite activities, then you will love Hyderabad in Telangana! Attracting both museum and art gallery enthusiasts, shopaholics, green parks lovers and night owls, this is indeed a pleasant destination that is sure to please all travellers, young and old alike. Its proximity to other top Indian destinations will also allow you, if you so wish, to combine your trip to Hyderabad with other visits, for a holiday full of discoveries.

Hyderabad has an eclectic mix of architecture which makes it fascinating to navigate around. From the rough-around-the-edges old town, to the luxury palaces of by gone kings, right through to the ultramodern buildings of the newly formed ‘HITEC City’, there is a style for everyone. You’ll want to make sure your camera is full charged for this city!. Charminar a monument and mosque built around 1591, is the focal point of Hyderabad. There are many theories of why it was built, but one of the most widely accepted is that it was to commemorate the eradication of the plague. Today it is open to the public and you can climb up the tower steps to get 360 degree sweeping views across the city, with the bustling market beneath.

India lends itself perfectly to road trips, so why not take advantage?
